# The World of Sylvaris

## Setting Overview

Sylvaris is a large continent containing diverse biomes, ancient resonance sites, corporate city-states, wilderness regions, and mythic ecological zones.

The world exists in a solarpunk-inspired era where advanced mirror technology coexists uneasily with ancient spiritual traditions.

## Major Biomes

- forests
- rivers
- beaches
- underwater regions
- mountains
- canyons
- deserts
- tundras
- volcanic regions

## Resonance

Resonance is the spiritual synchronization between humans, creatures, and ecosystems.

Ancient resonance bonds once allowed coexistence between humans and biome spirits.

## Mirror Technology

Mirror devices force creatures into obedience and enable scalable civilization growth.

Advantages:
- infrastructure
- transportation
- military stability
- modernization

Consequences:
- ecological damage
- spiritual disconnection
- corruption
- biome instability

## The Ancient Pact

The Aurali tribe maintained a sacred pact with biome spirits.

Children were paired with spirit companions through resonance amulets inherited through family lineages.

The youngest child inherits the family amulet because they have the greatest stake in the future.

## The Amulets

Amulets:
- enable resonance bonding
- contain inherited lineage
- are increasingly rare
- slowly disappeared after the ancient tribal split

The player inherits a damaged amulet corrupted generations earlier.

## The Airship

The player eventually builds a unique personal airship that serves as:
- home
- sanctuary
- crafting hub
- exploration platform
- mobile narrative space

No other character possesses an airship like the player’s.
