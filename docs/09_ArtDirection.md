# Art Direction

## Core Visual Philosophy

Sylvaris should feel:
- ancient
- ecological
- emotionally atmospheric
- spiritually alive
- beautiful but dangerous

The world should visually communicate:
- coexistence
- imbalance
- emotional resonance
- environmental consequence

## Art Style

Target style:
- stylized realism
- cinematic lighting
- painterly environments
- expressive silhouettes
- detailed atmosphere

Avoid:
- hyperrealism
- overly cartoonish proportions
- generic fantasy clutter
- MMO visual overload

## Visual Inspirations

Primary inspirations:
- Horizon Zero Dawn
- Princess Mononoke
- Breath of the Wild
- Shadow of the Colossus
- Journey
- Nausicaä of the Valley of the Wind

## Color Philosophy

### Healthy Ecosystems
- vibrant greens
- glowing blues
- warm sunlight
- bioluminescent accents
- soft atmospheric fog

### Strained Ecosystems
- muted colors
- unstable lighting
- harsh contrast
- environmental haze

### Corrupted Ecosystems
- desaturated regions
- resonance distortion
- environmental fractures
- visual silence
- unnatural glow patterns

## Resonance Visual Language

Resonance should appear:
- flowing
- organic
- layered
- emotionally reactive

Visual motifs:
- spirit threads
- glowing geometric lines
- flowing particles
- biome-linked elemental effects

## Mirror Visual Language

Mirror technology should appear:
- industrial
- angular
- compressed
- efficient
- rigid

Visual motifs:
- hard edges
- tactical overlays
- metallic framing
- constrained energy

## Creature Design Philosophy

Creatures should:
- feel ecological
- possess readable silhouettes
- communicate emotion visually
- reflect biome identity

Avoid:
- random monster complexity
- excessive armor plating
- overdesigned fantasy aesthetics

## Biome Design Philosophy

Each biome should:
- possess unique mood
- affect traversal
- alter emotional tone
- shape creature behavior

## Architectural Styles

### Aurali Villages
- living architecture
- woven natural materials
- resonance-integrated structures
- ecological integration

### Corporate Cities
- layered infrastructure
- airship towers
- mirror-powered transit
- industrial elegance

### Frontier Settlements
- practical survival design
- mixed technologies
- improvised structures

## Airship Design Philosophy

The player’s airship should feel:
- handcrafted
- personal
- modular
- emotionally lived-in

Visual themes:
- hanging lanterns
- resonance engines
- layered fabrics
- ecological technology

## UI Philosophy

The UI should feel:
- minimal
- contextual
- immersive
- emotionally integrated

### Resonance UI
Organic and flowing.

### Mirror UI
Angular and tactical.

### Hybrid UI
Visually unstable and conflicting.

## Mythic Resonance Visuals

Mythic resonance should feel:
- reality-breaking
- overwhelming
- emotionally dangerous

Effects:
- screen distortion
- elemental overflow
- environmental warping
- atmospheric instability

## Environmental Storytelling

The world should communicate history through:
- ruins
- ecosystem scars
- abandoned infrastructure
- creature migration
- weather patterns
- resonance anomalies
