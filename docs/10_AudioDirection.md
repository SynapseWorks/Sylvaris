# Audio Direction

## Core Audio Philosophy

Audio in Sylvaris should:
- emotionally ground the world
- reinforce ecological presence
- create spiritual atmosphere
- communicate danger and wonder

The world should sound alive.

## Audio Pillars

- atmosphere
- creature emotion
- resonance identity
- environmental storytelling
- silence as tension

## Music Direction

Music style:
- orchestral
- ambient
- tribal
- ecological
- emotional
- atmospheric

Blend:
- organic instrumentation
- resonance textures
- subtle electronic layers

## Musical Inspirations

- Journey
- Princess Mononoke
- NieR: Automata
- Breath of the Wild
- Shadow of the Colossus

## Biome Audio Identity

Each biome should possess:
- unique ambience
- creature soundscape
- resonance tone
- weather personality

## Resonance Audio

Resonance should sound:
- harmonic
- layered
- emotional
- slightly alive

Effects:
- whispers
- harmonic pulses
- environmental reverb
- emotional tone shifts

## Mirror Audio

Mirror systems should sound:
- compressed
- synthetic
- constrained
- industrial

## Creature Audio Philosophy

Creatures should communicate emotion through:
- tone
- rhythm
- environmental interaction
- subtle vocalization

Avoid:
- generic monster sounds
- repetitive combat audio

## Silence

Silence is extremely important.

Dead ecosystems should feel:
- empty
- spiritually disconnected
- emotionally disturbing

The absence of creature sounds should create tension.

## Mythic Audio

Mythic events should sound:
- overwhelming
- environmental
- enormous
- reality-distorting

Examples:
- distant thunder
- layered resonance
- low-frequency pressure
- biome-wide echoes

## Weather Audio

Weather should strongly affect:
- exploration mood
- traversal feel
- creature activity
- emotional tone

## Spirit Companion Audio

The spirit companion should sound:
- soft
- emotionally expressive
- comforting
- ancient

Communication may evolve from:
- tones
- whispers
- resonance impressions
- partial language

## Dynamic Audio Systems

Audio should react dynamically to:
- corruption
- resonance use
- ecosystem stability
- faction presence
- creature behavior
- player mythic adaptation

## Airship Audio

The player’s airship should feel:
- cozy
- isolated
- atmospheric

Sounds:
- engine hum
- wind creaks
- resonance pulses
- hanging lanterns
- distant storms
