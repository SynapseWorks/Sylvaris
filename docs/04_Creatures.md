# Creatures

## Creature Philosophy

Creatures in Sylvaris are not collectible monsters.

They are:
- ecological spirit-animal hybrids
- emotionally intelligent beings
- biome-linked entities
- philosophical actors within the world

Every creature should feel like:
- part of an ecosystem
- emotionally reactive
- spiritually meaningful
- capable of agency

## Creature Roles

Each creature functions simultaneously as:
- ecological participant
- combat companion
- traversal enhancer
- narrative symbol
- emotional relationship

## Species Resonance

Creatures of the same species share emotional resonance impressions.

Helping or harming one creature influences how others of the species initially react to the player.

This is not a hive mind, but a biome-linked emotional memory system.

## Creature Relationship States

Suggested relationship progression:
- Wary
- Curious
- Familiar
- Trusting
- Resonant
- Deeply Bonded
- Mythically Attuned

Relationships may strengthen or deteriorate over time depending on player actions.

## Creature Emotional States

Creatures may experience:
- fear
- curiosity
- exhaustion
- aggression
- grief
- territoriality
- protectiveness
- instability
- playfulness

## Creature Categories

Suggested ecological creature families:
- River Spirits
- Storm Creatures
- Canyon Predators
- Forest Guardians
- Volcanic Entities
- Fungal Networks
- Ocean Spirits
- Tundra Beings

## Creature Intelligence Levels

### Tier 1 — Instinctual
Simple ecological behavior.

### Tier 2 — Social
Pack/herd/group dynamics.

### Tier 3 — Resonant
Can understand emotional intent and bond deeply.

### Tier 4 — Mythic
Ancient ecosystem-level entities.

## Mythic Creatures

Mythic creatures:
- are ecosystem anchors
- affect climate and ecosystems
- may retaliate against ecological collapse
- are feared by civilization

Wild creatures may trigger mythic resonance independently to defend their biome.

## Creature Death And Rebirth

Creatures do not permanently die traditionally.

They:
- return to biome resonance
- regenerate spiritually over time
- reform through ecological cycles

Extreme corruption or ecosystem collapse may permanently alter species.

## Corruption

Corruption affects:
- behavior
- visuals
- resonance
- ecosystems
- emotional stability

Corrupted creatures should feel tragic rather than evil.

## The Spritedex

The Spritedex functions as:
- ecological field journal
- creature research archive
- emotional observation log

Information is unlocked through:
- observation
- bonding
- exploration
- lore discovery
- creature interaction

Entries include:
- sketches
- emotional impressions
- ecosystem notes
- myths
- likes/dislikes
- goals
- behavior patterns

## The Spirit Companion

The player’s spirit companion:
- is permanent
- evolves emotionally
- changes visually based on player philosophy
- enables resonance vision
- maintains the damaged amulet connection
- acts as emotional and narrative anchor

The spirit companion may represent the final intact bridge between creatures and humanity.
