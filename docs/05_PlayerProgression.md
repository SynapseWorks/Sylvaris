# Player Progression

## Progression Philosophy

Progression in Sylvaris is based on:
- identity
- relationships
- exploration
- ecological understanding
- philosophy
- resonance growth

The player should not become powerful through grinding alone.

## Core Progression Systems

### 1. Human Growth
Traversal, crafting, survival, diplomacy, combat.

### 2. Resonance Growth
Spiritual synchronization and fusion capability.

### 3. Creature Bond Growth
Relationship progression with bonded creatures.

### 4. Spirit Companion Evolution
Emotional and visual evolution tied to player choices.

### 5. World Reputation
Faction, creature, biome, and public perception systems.

## Resonance Tiers

Suggested progression:
- Unawakened
- Attuned
- Harmonized
- Deepbound
- Mythic

## Skill Path Philosophy

Players are not hard-locked into classes.

Identity emerges through actions and specialization.

## Suggested Skill Paths

### Harmony Path
Healing, restoration, stabilization.

### Wildbond Path
Aggressive fusion and primal resonance.

### Wayfinder Path
Traversal and exploration mastery.

### Artifice Path
Crafting, engineering, mirror integration.

### Veilcraft Path
Stealth, secrecy, forbidden resonance.

## Mythic Resonance

Mythic resonance:
- massively increases power
- alters ecosystems
- affects public perception
- creates environmental instability

Creatures must return to their biome to recover after extreme mythic use.

## Mythic Adaptation

Repeated mythic bonding permanently alters the player:
- movement
- appearance
- instincts
- emotional tendencies
- biome affinity

The player gradually becomes less entirely human.

## Social Consequences

Frequent mythic usage may cause:
- fear
- worship
- political hostility
- military surveillance
- public panic

## Mirror Progression

Mirror users gain:
- tactical flexibility
- large creature access
- scalable combat

But lose:
- emotional synchronization
- deep resonance
- true mythic fusion

## Hybrid Progression

Players may combine:
- mirror systems
- resonance systems

This creates:
- unstable abilities
- political controversy
- unique gameplay paths

## Traversal Progression

Traversal evolves through creature bonds:
- current riding
- gliding
- resonance climbing
- thermal movement
- wall traversal
- environmental adaptation

Movement itself is progression.

## Airship Progression

The personal airship evolves through:
- upgrades
- player philosophy
- resonance systems
- mirror integrations
- ecological adaptations

The airship acts as:
- home
- sanctuary
- narrative hub
- mobile exploration base
