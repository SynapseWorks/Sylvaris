# Factions

## The Aurali

Ancient resonance tribe.

Beliefs:
- coexistence
- stewardship
- spiritual responsibility

Strengths:
- resonance bonding
- ecological knowledge
- mythic synchronization

Weaknesses:
- isolationism
- dwindling numbers
- inherited shame

---

## Dominion Consortium

Corporate civilization powered by mirror infrastructure.

Beliefs:
- stability
- progress
- scalable systems

Strengths:
- cities
- medicine
- airships
- infrastructure

Weaknesses:
- ecological extraction
- spiritual disconnection

---

## Ashen Vanguard

Militarized descendants of ancient conflicts.

Beliefs:
- power must be controlled
- resonance is dangerous

Strengths:
- tactical combat
- mirror warfare
- discipline

Weaknesses:
- authoritarianism
- escalation politics

---

## Wildbound

Eco-radical groups attempting to reclaim the world for nature.

Beliefs:
- civilization is destroying ecosystems
- mythic retaliation is necessary

Strengths:
- sabotage
- creature alliances
- ecological warfare

Weaknesses:
- extremism
- civilian casualties

Wildbound groups may intentionally trigger mythic biome retaliation as ecological terrorism.

---

## Veil Market

Smugglers, black markets, and opportunists.

Beliefs:
- power belongs to those who can use it

Strengths:
- underground networks
- forbidden technology
- flexibility

Weaknesses:
- instability
- betrayal
- corruption spread
