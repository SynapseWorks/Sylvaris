# Sylvaris — Vision

## High Concept

Sylvaris is an open-world creature-bond RPG where players explore a living solarpunk fantasy world shaped by ecology, political conflict, mythic resonance, and emotional creature relationships.

Players form bonds with spirit-creatures tied to biomes and ecosystems while navigating competing ideologies surrounding humanity’s relationship with nature and power.

## The Fantasy

The player should feel like:
- a wandering mythic explorer
- a bridge between humanity and nature
- a spiritual traveler
- someone discovering their place in the world
- someone whose choices genuinely shape ecosystems and societies

## Core Emotional Tone

- beautiful
- adventurous
- emotionally grounded
- mysterious
- melancholic
- hopeful
- occasionally terrifying

## Core Design Philosophy

The world should feel:
- alive
- reactive
- ancient
- emotionally meaningful
- ecologically believable

The player should never fully control nature.

Power should always carry:
- ecological consequences
- social consequences
- emotional consequences

## Main Narrative Question

Can humanity coexist with living power without destroying either itself or nature?

## Player Identity Philosophy

The player is not a traditional “chosen one.”

The player becomes important because:
- old systems are failing
- the resonance pact is collapsing
- their inherited amulet is damaged
- circumstance created new possibilities

The player becomes a catalyst within a changing world.
