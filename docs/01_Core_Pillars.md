# Core Pillars

## 1. Relationship Over Ownership

Creatures are intelligent ecological beings and companions, not collectible inventory objects.

Bonding requires:
- trust
- philosophy alignment
- shared experiences
- ecological respect

## 2. Power Has Consequences

Mythic resonance and large-scale power affect:
- ecosystems
- weather
- faction politics
- public perception
- creature stability

## 3. Exploration Is Progression

Players advance primarily through:
- discovery
- travel
- creature encounters
- ecological understanding
- lore
- world interaction

## 4. The World Is Alive

Sylvaris contains:
- evolving ecosystems
- faction conflict
- migration systems
- dynamic events
- corruption spread
- mythic reactions

The world continues evolving even without player involvement.

## 5. Identity Is Chosen

Players may:
- join any faction
- balance conflicting ideologies
- become extremists
- become mediators
- combine resonance and mirror systems

No route is presented as fully correct.

## 6. Nature Is Beautiful And Terrifying

Nature in Sylvaris is:
- emotionally powerful
- spiritually alive
- unpredictable
- sometimes catastrophic

Mythic creatures function as ecosystem-level forces.
